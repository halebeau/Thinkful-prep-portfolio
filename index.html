A little bit about me.

I was born and raised in San Diego. For the last 20 years I've succeeded as a hair stylist, which has given me superior client relationship skills and a trained eye for trends, fashions and allure. My journey into tech started in Los Angeles though, when I got a job in Post-Production and became a 'Video Restoration Artist'. After tha I went on to becoming a 'Stereographic Composition Artist'. So as you can guess, I've spent many many hours behind a computer cracking down a deadline.

Though it was the artistic side of computer work, I've always been interested in how it old got done behind the scenes. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs. That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first webpage, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
